Origin ‘Theme Song’ Part 2

 

 

So we’re continuing the ‘Origin theme Song’ with Part 2 and I have quite a lot lined up!

Last time I told you what song I think fits the bill for the book overall.

 

This time I want to address songs I think suit our main protagonists, Ken and Viera.

 

 

Let’s start with Captain Kennedy Jackson J. He’s a serious piece of work sometimes (what am I saying? I mean all the time) and his character—like all of the JJ men—is tormented by his past and seeks to make a better future. Nothing wrong with that, right?

 

I’m choosing ‘Up In The Air’ by Thirty Seconds to Mars for Ken’s theme. I’m an unhealthy fan of 30 Seconds to Mars and they’ve rocked my socks since the very beginning. This song helped me A LOT when getting into Ken’s mind, it helped me understand him better and evidently—fall in love with him. Up in The Air underlines every struggle, thought or crazy idea Ken has ever had. I think it’s safe to say that we have a winner.

 

 

Last and most certainly not least, we have Viera. Our female heart/ball breaker.

I think she’s probably one of the most in depth and intimidating female characters I’ve ever written. I mean—if I was to see this chick coming at me down the street, I guarantee 50 percent of me would cross the road or flatten myself against a wall to make myself invisible. (No but let’s be real for a moment, I love her. She’s alright *grins*)

Without a doubt in my body. Viera’s theme is ‘Unbreakable’ by Fireflight.

The lyrics say everything about her as a person, from her aggressive tendencies to her cute blushing ears. She’s a survivor by nature.

 

 

Combined, Ken and Viera are a dynamic duo. It’s partially why they work well together and why, at times, they could kill each other. We know they get their HEA (Happily Ever After) and if there’s ever a song I could pick for these two without it appearing whimsical where the sky rains roses, it would be ‘Stay Close’ by Fireflight.

They are no in way possible a ‘normal’ couple and I think this particular song says it all.
